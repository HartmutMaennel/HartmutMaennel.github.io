Krull dimension

Supremum of the lengths of all chains of prime ideals (the length is the number of strict inclusions, not the number of primes; these differ by 1).

Wikipedia

A field has dimension 0.
A PID that is not a field has dimension 1.
An integral extension of a ring has the same dimension as the ring does.

Local rings

A commutative ring is local if and only if it has a unique maximal ideal.
Wikipedia

Regular Local Ring

A regular local ring is a Noetherian local ring having the property that the minimal number of generators of its maximal ideal is equal to its Krull dimension.
